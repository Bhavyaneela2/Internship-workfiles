Jobs at UDAAN Society

JOB OPPORTUNITY: Block Coordinator for WASH Program (Amroha, Uttar Pradesh)

Are you passionate about making a difference in Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) and empowering communities?

UDAAN Society, a leading NGO with over 21 years of experience in WASH and community development, is seeking dedicated and experienced professionals for a transformative WASH program in Uttar Pradesh. We are looking for:

  • 3 Block Coordinator

(Location: Amroha District, Uttar Pradesh)

About the Project:

This vital partnership, aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 5 and 6, aims to “Convene, Converge and Catalyse resources for system strengthening of sustainable WASH programmes with a focus on marginalised and vulnerable communities of Uttar Pradesh.” The project supports the Government of India and Uttar Pradesh’s Swachh Bharat Mission, Grameen (SBM-G) and Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) to ensure all households and institutions in rural Uttar Pradesh have access to improved and climate-resilient WASH facilities and services. A key focus is on addressing critical gaps in safely managed sanitation, drinking water, and gender discrimination in WASH.

Key Responsibilities (General, Specifics will vary by role):

  • Facilitate and coordinate program activities in close collaboration with UNICEF and state/district administrative units (SBM-G, JJM, School and Anganwadi Kayakalp etc.).
  • Support capacity building initiatives for various stakeholders including government functionaries, communities, and local institutions.
  • Implement evidence-based advocacy to realize children’s and women’s rights to water and sanitation.
  • Ensure women’s participation (at least one-third) in all planning, implementation, monitoring, and execution meetings.
  • Actively involve Elected Women Representatives in community-level engagements.
  • Promote inclusive representation of women and girls from all community sections in decision-making processes.
  • Support the achievement of project outputs related to safely managed sanitation, drinking water, and WASH in institutions.
  • Contribute to gender-responsive WASH programming, including Menstrual Health and Hygiene Management (MHHM) sensitization for adolescent girls and boys.
  • Ensure compliance with Prevention of Sexual Exploitation and Abuse (PSEA) guidelines.

Desired Profile:

  • Proven experience in the WASH sector, community mobilization, and system strengthening.
  • Understanding of government flagship programs like SBM-G and JJM.
  • Strong commitment to gender equality and social inclusion in WASH programming.
  • Excellent communication, coordination, and training skills.
  • Ability to work effectively with government officials, communities, and diverse stakeholders.
  • Familiarity with the local language (Hindi) is essential.

Project Duration:

The partnership aims to achieve its objectives by June 2026, with overall project goals extending to 2027.

How to Apply:

Please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your relevant experience and why you are a suitable candidate for this role to mail@udaansociety.org by 15/2/2026 with Subject Line: Application for the Post of Block Coordinator.

Join us in making a sustainable impact on WASH services and empowering communities in Uttar Pradesh.
